Application Guide

Where to Apply

Pulse points amplify fragrance as body heat helps diffuse the perfume throughout your day.

Neck & Décolletage

The warmest pulse point — ideal for the very first impression of your scent on others.

Inner Wrists

A classic placement that projects heart notes gracefully throughout the day.

Inner Elbows

Soft skin here enables deeper base note absorption and slow, rich diffusion.

Behind the Knees

Creates a gentle trail that rises with movement, leaving a lasting impression.

✦

Do not rub wrists together — this crushes the top notes and alters the fragrance's natural evolution.
